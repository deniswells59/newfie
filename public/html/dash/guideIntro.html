What's a Guide?

A Guide tours guests and helps ensure they enjoy their time, wherever they are. A guide should specialize in activities that they also enjoy, and share an experience with others.

What You're Willing to Do

Pick Locations

Pick an area you know well. Somewhere you've been before or (even better) where you live. This is the place you're offering to escort people who probably don't know what's good

Choose Your Expertise

Decide on one or two interests that you can excel at. Do you know the best places to eat? Or maybe the best clubs that stay open late? Whatever you know, travelers will see the value in it.

Plan the trip

Just know what the hell you're doing! Have an itinerary or wing it. As long as the guest knows what's what.
